# ADVANCED OPPORTUNITY LAYER

## PURPOSE
Identify and prioritise high-value opportunities with maximum leverage.

## SCAN SOURCES
- market gaps
- inefficiencies
- repetitive tasks
- user pain points
- emerging trends

## EVALUATION CRITERIA

Score each (1–5):

- demand
- ease of build
- monetisation potential
- competition
- speed to launch

## OPPORTUNITY SCORE

Total = sum of all scores

## PRIORITY

High → build immediately  
Medium → refine  
Low → discard  

## OUTPUT

- top 3 opportunities
- reasoning
- recommended action
